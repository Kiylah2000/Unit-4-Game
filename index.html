How to Play:


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. There are 4 diamonds below and each diamond is assigned a random number. By clicking on a diamond, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score is greater than the random number. Good Luck!!
